What Is Mumbai 3.0?

Mumbai 3.0 is an upcoming planned urban region in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region (MMR), being developed under the Government of Maharashtra and MMRDA.

It is strategically located between Mumbai and Navi Mumbai, mainly spread across the Uran, Panvel, and Pen belt, covering around 124 villages. With major infrastructure projects like the Navi Mumbai International Airport, Atal Setu, and upcoming connectivity corridors, this region is set to become the next growth hub.

Mumbai 3.0 represents the future expansion of Mumbai, offering better planning, improved connectivity, and strong investment potential.
