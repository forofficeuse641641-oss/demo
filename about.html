In Maharashtra, land conversations don't start with location or price. They start with one simple question: "Saatbara clear hai na?"

Because that's where the real story of the land is written. Ownership, details, any issues — everything shows up there first.

We didn't want a fancy name. We chose something people already understand and rely on. For us, selling land isn't just about showing plots — it's about getting the basics right. Because in the end, everything begins with the 7/12.

The 7/12 Extract — Explained
"Saatbara
clear hai na?"

The 7/12 (Satbara Utara) is Maharashtra's official land record extract. No matter how good something looks on the outside, people here trust what's written on the 7/12.

It captures the owner's name, survey number, area, crop details, water source, and any encumbrances or court orders attached to the land.

Before any plot leaves our hands, we've verified the 7/12, cross-checked the 8A mutation register, and confirmed the title chain is completely clean.
